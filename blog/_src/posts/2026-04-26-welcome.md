This is the first post on the yrdsl.app blog. Hi.

I'm building [yrdsl.app](https://yrdsl.app) in the open — a digital yard sale you can either run yourself (self-host on GitHub Pages, Vercel, Netlify, or Cloudflare Pages — all free) or let us host for pennies a month. Drop in your photos and Claude writes the listings; share a single short URL; buyers reserve what they want. The whole thing is open source, MIT-licensed, lives at [github.com/KuvopLLC/yrdsl](https://github.com/KuvopLLC/yrdsl).

## What's going to live here

A few things I want to write about as the project grows:

- **Build progress.** Short weekly-ish notes on what shipped, what broke, what I'm learning. Sometimes the post is the changelog; sometimes it's the story behind one ugly bug.
- **Sample sales.** Real yrdsl-built sales from the early users, with notes on what worked and what felt awkward. The plan is for these to double as inspiration when you're staring at an empty editor.
- **How-tos.** Concrete walkthroughs: setting up the [Claude Code plugin](https://github.com/KuvopLLC/yrdsl/tree/main/plugins/yrdsl), self-hosting on each of the four supported platforms, building a WhatsApp bot on top of the API, that kind of thing.
- **Behind the curtain.** The architecture, the trade-offs I picked, the things I'd do differently. For developers and curious users who want to understand how the sausage is made.

There won't be a posting schedule. I'll write when there's something worth saying, not when it's been a week.

## How to follow along

- **RSS**: [feed.xml](/blog/feed.xml) — drop into Feedly, NetNewsWire, whatever you use.
- **Reddit**: [r/yrdsl](https://www.reddit.com/r/yrdsl/) — the community sub. New posts get linked there too. It's also where I listen for feedback on the hosted product.
- **GitHub**: [KuvopLLC/yrdsl](https://github.com/KuvopLLC/yrdsl) — code, issues, PRs.
- **Email**: [matt@mreider.com](mailto:matt@mreider.com) for the private stuff.

If you've got a yard sale's worth of stuff to clear out, [give it a try](https://app.yrdsl.app/signup). Free during pre-release; we read every signup. See you in the next post.
